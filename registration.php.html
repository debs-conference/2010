Visa recommendation letters

Please send all visa support letter requests to supportletters@acm.org, with a CC to debs10-registration@doc.ic.ac.uk, and include all of the following information:

  1. Name as it appears on your passport
  2. Current Mailing address
  3. The name of the conference you wish to attend: DEBS'10
  4. If you have any papers accepted for the conference, please indicate whether you are the sole author or a co-author, and provide the full title of the document.
  5. Registration confirmation number
